Facts about Rylee

  • She is approximately 9 years old
  • She is 3/4 Beagle and 1/4 Cavalier King Charles Spaniel
  • She loves walks
  • She love treats
  • She loves people
  • Her nose gets her into trouble
  • She loves food and dinner scraps

All about Rylee

Rylee is short for a beagle and has the traditional coloring with spots on her lower legs and belly. People think she is a puppy because of her size. Her nose is a trouble maker as when she gets a scent of something it leads her in that direction and she does not listen, so she always stays on leash. She does not like cats, she does not socialize with other dogs, but tolerates a younger golden retriever named Sam. When it comes to food, she likes just about anything. She eats until she runs out of food, but would continue to eat if she could. She likes to sleep, a lot. She spends a good amount of time lying down when she is not walking or begging for food.
